Basic First Aid

Essential first aid steps for common emergencies

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

Check for responsiveness

Tap shoulders and shout "Are you okay?"

Call 108

If unresponsive, call emergency services immediately

Start chest compressions

Place hands on center of chest, push hard and fast (100-120 compressions/minute)

Give rescue breaths

Tilt head back, pinch nose, give 2 breaths (if trained)

Continue until help arrives

Alternate 30 compressions with 2 breaths

Heimlich Maneuver

Recognize choking

Person cannot speak, cough, or breathe

Stand behind person

Place feet shoulder-width apart for stability

Make fist with one hand

Place thumb side against abdomen above navel

Grasp fist with other hand

Press into abdomen with quick upward thrusts

Repeat until object is expelled

Continue until person can breathe or becomes unconscious
