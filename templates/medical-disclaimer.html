Medical Disclaimer

Important information about the limitations of our AI medical assistant

CRITICAL NOTICE

MedAI is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of qualified healthcare providers with questions about medical conditions.

1. Nature of Service

What MedAI Provides:

  • General health information and education
  • AI-powered responses to health-related questions
  • Access to medical resources and references
  • Emergency contact information and guidance

What MedAI Does NOT Provide:

  • Medical diagnosis or treatment recommendations
  • Professional medical advice
  • Replacement for doctor-patient consultations
  • Emergency medical services

2. Limitations of AI Technology

AI Limitations

  • Cannot perform physical examinations
  • Cannot access your medical history
  • May not recognize all symptoms
  • Cannot provide personalized treatment plans

Information Accuracy

  • Information may not be current
  • AI responses are based on training data
  • May not cover all medical conditions
  • Cannot replace clinical judgment

3. When to Seek Professional Help

Always Consult Healthcare Professionals For:

  • Any new or worsening symptoms
  • Persistent pain or discomfort
  • Changes in existing conditions
  • Medication questions or side effects
  • Preventive care and screenings
  • Mental health concerns

4. Emergency Situations

In Case of Emergency

Do NOT rely on MedAI for emergency medical situations

Call 108

For immediate medical assistance

5. User Responsibility

By Using MedAI, You Acknowledge:

  • You understand the limitations of AI technology
  • You will not use MedAI as a substitute for professional medical care
  • You will seek appropriate medical attention when needed
  • You understand that medical information changes frequently
  • You will verify important information with healthcare providers

6. Liability Disclaimer

MedAI and its operators are not liable for:

  • Any medical decisions made based on AI responses
  • Delays in seeking professional medical care
  • Misinterpretation of AI-provided information
  • Any health outcomes related to use of this service
  • Technical errors or service interruptions

7. Best Practices

✅ Do:

  • Use MedAI for general health education
  • Verify important information with doctors
  • Keep regular appointments with healthcare providers
  • Use emergency services for urgent situations

❌ Don't:

  • Rely solely on AI for medical decisions
  • Ignore professional medical advice
  • Use MedAI for emergency situations
  • Self-diagnose based on AI responses

Remember

MedAI is a tool to supplement, not replace, professional medical care. Your health and safety are our top priorities, and we encourage you to always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.
