Training Process

How the model was developed and trained

Data Collection & Cleaning

Collected 256,916 doctor-patient conversations from medical forums. Applied advanced cleaning techniques to remove noise and standardize format.

Text Preprocessing

Used SpaCy for tokenization, lemmatization, and text normalization. Preserved medical terminology while standardizing format.

Embedding Generation

Leveraged Sentence Transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) to create 384-dimensional semantic embeddings for each patient question.

FAISS Indexing

Built high-performance FAISS index for sub-second similarity search across the entire dataset using cosine similarity.

Topic Modeling

Applied Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) to identify medical topics and enable category-based filtering and suggestions.
